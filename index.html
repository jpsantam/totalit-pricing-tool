Before we start

Standard, or co‑managed?

Standard: the bundle's line-up is fixed — every service shown is included and can't be removed, though you can still add extra services for this quote. Co-managed: some services are handled by the client or a third party, so you start from the bundle's usual line-up and choose which lines actually apply.

Question 1 of 4

Which bundle?

Essentials: helpdesk, patching and backup. Secure adds 24/7 cyber monitoring and response. Premium adds board-level cyber reporting and formal compliance/certification services (Cyber Essentials, ISO 27001 gap analysis).
